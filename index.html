About Visionary Youth Group

Visionary Youth Group is a member-driven financial community that provides savings plans, access to affordable loans, group investments and financial literacy programs for young people. We prioritize transparency, governance and measurable impact.

Our Mission

To empower youth economically through consistent savings, access to fair credit, and group investments that build generational wealth.

Our Vision

To be a leading youth-owned financial cooperative renowned for trust, growth and social impact.

Why Join Us

Savings Plans

Weekly and monthly buckets that suit your cashflow.

Affordable Loans

Short-term loans to help members in emergencies and small business needs.

Group Investments

Collective projects to grow member funds and share profits.

Financial literacy courses and entrepreneurship workshops.
