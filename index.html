About Us

VISIONARY YOUTH GROUP 2024 is a youth-driven initiative dedicated to empowering young people through financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and community development. Our mission is to create sustainable opportunities for youth while fostering unity and growth.

Information

Vision

To inspire and nurture a generation of empowered and self-reliant youth who are drivers of positive change in society.

Mission

To create opportunities for youth through education, innovation, and entrepreneurship while building a united and progressive community.

Objectives

  • Promote financial literacy and savings among members.
  • Support entrepreneurship and skill development.
  • Engage in community projects that uplift livelihoods.
  • Foster teamwork, accountability, and leadership among youth.

Activities

  • Monthly member contributions and savings programs.
  • Business mentorship and training sessions.
  • Community service and outreach programs.
  • Youth empowerment seminars and events.
